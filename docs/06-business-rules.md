# Business Rules

## Purpose
This document captures business rules for the Club Zilla Digital Platform. Each rule must be labeled as `Proposed`, `Approved`, `Rejected`, or `Needs clarification`.

## Status definitions
- **Proposed:** Suggested for stakeholder review.
- **Approved:** Confirmed by authorized stakeholders.
- **Rejected:** Reviewed and excluded from scope.
- **Needs clarification:** Requires additional information before a decision.

## Business rules register
| ID | Rule | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| BR-001 | Unknown information must be marked as `TBD` in project documentation. | Approved | Required documentation convention. |
| BR-002 | Agents and contributors must not invent requirements for the Club Zilla Digital Platform. | Approved | Requirements must come from approved sources or be marked as TBD. |
| BR-003 | Production development must not start until the MVP, BRD, SRS, and architecture are approved. | Approved | Applies to backend, frontend, mobile, infrastructure, and test code. |
| BR-004 | Business rules must be labeled as `Proposed`, `Approved`, `Rejected`, or `Needs clarification`. | Approved | Required for clarity during discovery. |
| BR-005 | The MVP scope must be approved by authorized stakeholders before implementation planning. | Proposed | Approval process is TBD. |
| BR-006 | User roles and permissions must be validated before access control design is finalized. | Proposed | Role model is TBD. |
| BR-007 | Data privacy, retention, and compliance requirements must be identified before architecture approval. | Needs clarification | Applicable regulations are TBD. |
| BR-008 | Integration requirements must be confirmed before SRS approval. | Needs clarification | Required integrations are TBD. |
