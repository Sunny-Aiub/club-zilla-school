# User Roles

## Purpose
This document captures initial user role terminology for the Club Zilla Digital Platform. Role definitions, permissions, and workflows are discovery-stage assumptions unless approved.

## Role status labels
- **Proposed:** Suggested for discussion and validation.
- **Approved:** Confirmed by authorized stakeholders.
- **Rejected:** Explicitly excluded by authorized stakeholders.
- **Needs clarification:** Requires more information before acceptance or rejection.

## Initial user roles
| Role | Description | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| School Administrator | A Club Zilla School staff member responsible for managing platform configuration or operational oversight. | Needs clarification | Exact responsibilities are TBD. |
| Staff Member | A Club Zilla School staff member who may use the platform for daily school-related activities. | Needs clarification | Exact responsibilities are TBD. |
| Parent or Guardian | A parent or guardian associated with a student. | Needs clarification | Whether this role is in MVP is TBD. |
| Student | A learner associated with Club Zilla School. | Needs clarification | Whether students directly access the platform is TBD. |
| Appartisans Lab Team Member | A member of the development company supporting discovery, requirements, implementation, or delivery. | Proposed | Platform access needs are TBD. |
